Trust & safety

Built with child safety and educator trust at the core

Every AI feature is designed around one principle: educators review everything before it reaches a child or family.

Educator-reviewed AI
All AI-generated content is drafted for the educator to review and edit. Nothing goes to families without your approval.
Child data safety
We never store identifiable child information. Documentation uses age groups and general descriptors only.
Canadian privacy compliance
Built in line with PIPEDA and provincial childcare guidelines. Data stays in Canada.
Licensed daycares only
We onboard only licensed, regulated childcare providers. Every partner is verified before publishing.
